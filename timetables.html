Timetable

A perfect workout schedule is one that fits into your diet and life style and physical capabilities perfectly. A perfect workout schedule does not exist in the real world. There is no perfect schedule. Eventually your body will get used to any workout schedule and at that point, you need to bring about a sufficient level of variety into your workout regime to maintain progress.
A good workout schedule however is a real thing. It will be based on the principle of progressive overload. It will take into consideration your present physical state, weight, stamina and body weight. It will also take into consideration your goals, the way your body reacts to the extra stress and the history of your injuries.
